How to play

Story

You are Baron Von Grihznackh. Proud owner of both a brand new castle, and an enormous financial debt. The castle itself was mad expensive, but at least you got the land quite cheap, something about an monster infestation, but who really reads the entire contract anyways. At any rate, if some monsters would indeed happen to show up, you could simply don your marvelous top hat, and charge headfirst into glorious battle!

Gameplay

The monsters must not be allowed to destroy your castle. You loose when the castle reaches 0 HP

Attack the monsters to destroy them. Some monsters require more hits to die than others, but be careful, if the monsters touch you, you will get stunned for a short time.

Keybindings

Left arrow Move left
Right arrow Move right
Up arrow Jump
Z Attack left
X Attack right
