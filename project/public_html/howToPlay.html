How to play

The monsters must not be allowed to destroy your castle.

Attack the monsters to destroy them. Some monsters require more hits to die than others.

Be careful, if the monsters touch you, you will get stunned for a short time.

Keybindings

Left arrow Move left
Right arrow Move right
Up arrow Jump
Z Attack left
X Attack right
